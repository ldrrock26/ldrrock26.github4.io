Welcome to the Super Mario Hub

This site is all about the world of Super Mario. Super Mario is a long-running game series with famous characters and colorful style. This page is made for new Mario fans to get a grasp of what Super Mario is.

Super Mario Game Series

Use the buttons to filter the game cards and quickly explore different parts of the series.

2D Classic

Super Mario Bros.

One of the most iconic platform games ever made, this was the first game of Mario that helped define the style of the side-scrolling Mario experience with jumping, pipes, mushrooms, and Bowser’s castle levels.

2D Classic

Super Mario Bros. 3

Known for its creative worlds and power-ups, this game expanded the Mario formula and is still remembered as one of the most beloved entries in the series. It is also the first game to introduce where you can select what levels and paths you want to take.

2D Classic

Super Mario World

This colorful SNES classic introduced Yoshi and brought a bigger map, hidden exits, and a smooth polished feel that made it a major part of Mario history. To this day it is considered one of the best Mario games of all time.

3D Adventure

Super Mario 64

A major turning point for the franchise, this game brought Mario into 3D and showed how exploration, movement, and open level design could work in a platformer.

3D Adventure

Super Mario Sunshine

With its vacation setting and water-based FLUDD mechanic, Sunshine gave Mario a very distinct identity and offered a more movement-focused approach.

3D Adventure

Super Mario Galaxy

Famous for its gravity-based gameplay and cinematic feeling, Galaxy added a more space-inspired style while still keeping the core Mario charm and movement. This game is considered one of the best 3D Mario games of all time.

Modern Highlight

Super Mario Odyssey

Odyssey mixed open exploration with creative movement and the capture mechanic, giving players many ways to travel, experiment, and collect moons across large kingdoms.

Modern Highlight

Super Mario Bros. Wonder

The newest side-scrolling entry with lively animation and surprising wonder effects, this game shows how Nintendo keeps classic Mario ideas feeling fresh.

Modern Highlight

Mario Spin-Off Games

Beyond platforming, Mario also appears in racing, party, sports, RPG, and puzzle games, helping the series stay wide-ranging and fun for many different types of players.

Random Mario Fact

Mario’s games have included platforming, racing, sports, party games, RPGs, and more.
