Character Carousel Slider

Use the buttons to move through the Mario character slides.

Mario

Mario is the main hero of the franchise and is known for platforming, rescuing others, and leading many of the series’ most famous adventures.

Luigi

Luigi is Mario’s brother and often appears as a loyal companion in the games, though he also stars in his own games and is known for his nervous but lovable personality.

Princess Peach

Peach is the leader of the Mushroom Kingdom and appears throughout many Mario games as the main objective of the game and also as a playable character.

Yoshi

Yoshi is one of Mario’s most famous allies and adds a friendly, colorful feel to the series with his helpful abilities and memorable design.

Bowser

Bowser is the major recurring villain of the series and is known for his power, his castles, and his constant rivalry with Mario.
