Mario Characters

This page focuses on several well-known characters from the Mario franchise. You can search by name and also filter the cards by type to make the page easier to use.

Hero

Mario

Mario is the main hero of the franchise and is known for platforming, rescuing others, and leading many of the series’ most famous adventures.

Hero

Luigi

Luigi is Mario’s brother and often appears as a loyal companion in the games, though he also stars in his own games and is known for his nervous but lovable personality.

Royal

Princess Peach

Peach is the leader of the Mushroom Kingdom and appears throughout many Mario games as the main objective of the game and also as a playable character.

Villain

Bowser

Bowser is the major recurring villain of the series and is known for his power, his castles, and his constant rivalry with Mario.

Ally

Yoshi

Yoshi is one of Mario’s most famous allies and adds a friendly, colorful feel to the series with his helpful abilities and memorable design.

Ally

Toad

Toad is a recurring Mushroom Kingdom character who often helps Mario with his missions.

Royal

Princess Daisy

Daisy brings a more energetic personality to the Mario cast. She mainly appears in the sports, racing, and party-style spin-offs of Mario games.

Royal

Rosalina

Rosalina is tied strongly to the Galaxy era and gives the series a calmer, more galactic side through her design and story presence.

Ally

Donkey Kong

Donkey Kong has a long Nintendo history connected to Mario and appears in a number of crossover titles, especially in the racing and party games. He is originally the first villain Mario ever fought in the famous arcade game named Donkey Kong.
